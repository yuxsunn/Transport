    These two bar charts show the distribution of travel distances by private vehicle on weekday and weekend, with classified trip purposes. As the amount of travel is significantly reduced beyond the peak range between 20km to 24.9km, it is implied that the residents who live within 25km from city are willing travel to city by private vehicle. Also, only a few survey respondents travel more than 100 km to city by private vehicle. The switch of travel purposes in weekday and weekend has also been identified.

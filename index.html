    • TRANSPORTATION IN MELBOURNE
    Melbourne is Australia's second largest city and the world's most livable city. The City of Melbourne is Victoria's commercial, administrative, cultural and entertainment hub. Well-organised and efficient transport facilities are of the first importance in the economic and social life of Melbourne City.
    With the growing population and rapid development of transportation, it is essential to examine the present pattern of transport and existing infrastructure to identify the problem area. This website provides an overview of urban transport and then explores the key factors associated with most popular transport method: By Private Vehicle. Successful and vibrant cities require high-quality transport links. By exploring the transport pattern, the information will assist local government for future urban development and transportation strategies.
